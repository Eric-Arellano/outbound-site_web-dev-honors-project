What We Do

Our Mission

To make sure that no one is alone when coming out of the closet by facilitating relevant and personalized mentorship.

The Closet Problem

At least 500 thousand youth are still in the closet, in the U.S. alone.

  • Closet often feels confusing, lonely, and scary
  • One in three LGBTQ youth don't have an adult they can trust
  • Allies often can't relate to the actual experience of coming out

Our Solution: Connection

Connect with a mentor who has been through a similar experience. Ask them questions, learn from their story, find another ally.

  • Matched for relevance to your interests, obstacles, and situation
  • Safe and confidential online communication
  • Non-judgemental and relevant outlet
